Rescue a Fused-to-Death-Attiny85

Di 10 September 2013
By x4343

In Misc.

tags: attiny85fuseshigh voltage programming

I accidentally destroyed my Attiny85. I wanted to use an external 16 MHz crystal to speed it up. But in a hurry I accidentally burned the bootloader for 20 MHz to it. So no way to reprogramm it without a 20 MHz crystal. I don't have one. But I found a very suitable solution to reset the Attiny to factory settings with so called 'high voltage programming'.
